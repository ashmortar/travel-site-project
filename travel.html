How to Get to Bahama Mama

Being a private resort we can only be reached by sea or air. Luckily for our exclusive guests both of these are available through our list of logistical specialists.

By Sea

Captain Roger Salamander runs a ferry that will bring to directly to the island from the nearest airport. He is known for his salty toungue and his delicious mimosas. We guarantee you will enjoy your journey with on The Salamander. You can book with him at 555-the-capn or via email at captain@rogersalamander.com

By Air

Former Air Force Ace Major Tom Hardy runs our seaplane service. He singlehandedly won the US the battle of Lagunitas and was a hero at the Bay of IPA. His gruff attitude is contagious, so don't let him breathe on you but his plane is the surefire fastest way to reach Bahama Mama. Flights can be arranged at 555-1FLY-GUY or via email at chopperrides@bahamamama.com
